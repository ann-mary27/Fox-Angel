Area of Focus

Amit is a seasoned transaction advisory professional with experience across various industries. Having joined EY in 1989, he has worked on several transactions across sectors, and served as a client service partner for various private equity and corporate accounts for EY.

Leading various inbound and outbound transaction advisory engagements, Amit actively advises private equity and strategic clients in India. He has worked extensively in the oil and gas, industrial and consumer products, and health care sectors.

Additionally, Amit has headed various due diligence projects for large steel companies under the Indian Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code, and leads various sell-side engagements including vendor due diligence and vendor assistance.

A member of various professional bodies including the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India and the Institute of Cost Accountants of India, he earned his BCom degree from Delhi University.
