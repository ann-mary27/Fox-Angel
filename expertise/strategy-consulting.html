Strategy Consulting

01

Corporate Level Strategy

Fox & angel will define the very foundation of your business and its purpose. Be it stability strategy, expansion strategy, retrenchment strategy or a combination of all, Fox & Angel will ensure that your corporate strategy is aligned with your vison and mission.

Several pertinent decisions pertaining but not limited to diversification, market penetration, liquidation, forward, backward or horizontal integration etc. are dwelled upon before giving you that winning strategy.

02

Business Level Strategy

While working on your Business level strategy, Fox & Angel will focus primarily on the competitive advantage that will enable your business to outperform competitors. This will be a more focussed strategy, which will provide answers to what your business can achieve through competitive price, product development or customer centricity etc.. Cost Leadership and Differentiation, the two most common types of business level strategies will be provide.

03

Functional Level Strategy

Fox & Angel shall provide the functional level strategy covering specific goals and actions required to achieve those goals. This strategy will determine the day-to-day functioning of your company and its various teams and departments such as R &D and Marketing.

04

GTM Strategy

The Go-To -Market strategy is used to bring a new product or service to a market and is designed to mitigate risk inherent to doing it. Creating a go-to-market strategy is as important for established businesses as it is for brand new entrepreneurial endeavours. This comprehensive plan will be provided by Fox & Angel and includes target market profiles, a concrete sales and distribution strategy and a marketing plan.

05

Restructuring Brands & Business

Changing a logo or the brand name may seem like an insignificant task but it can make or break a company if not done right. Brand or Business restructuring is usually done to modify its capital structure or its operations significantly. Generally, this happens when a brand or business entity is experiencing significant problems and is in financial jeopardy. This may also happen to keep up with the changing market landscape and newer opportunities. Fox & Angel has a team of experts who take care of all steps of this transition, leading to a positive and successful brand makeover.

06

Turn Around Strategy

Is your business in loss? Has there been a decline in your company’s performance? Fret not, industry experts of Fox & Angel have a strategic approach of restructuring and transforming companies from loss to profitability. We deploy a range of measures that help recover businesses from a period of a performance decline. These measures can be in the form of significant changes in the business activities, leadership and asset retrenchment.

07

Pricing Strategy

Are you wondering why your product isn’t selling? More often than not, it has to do with the price, which is why pricing strategy is one of the most crucial strategies to have. Fox & Angel helps you choose prices that maximize profits, revenue and shareholder values while considering market demand and consumer demand
